We Are TakawYaki

TakawYaki started with a simple love for Japanese street food and a passion for sharing delicious flavors. Inspired by the authentic takoyaki of Osaka, we set out to create our own version—crispy on the outside, soft and flavorful inside. From a small idea to a thriving business, TakawYaki is now a go-to spot for takoyaki lovers, bringing people together one bite at a time.
